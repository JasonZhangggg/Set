How to Play

Find sets of 3 elements

In the game, certain combinations of three cards are said to make up a "set". For all the categories of features:

  • NUMBER: 1, 2, 3
  • SHAPE: Diamond, Oval, Squiggle
  • SHADING: Solid, Striped, Empty
  • COLOR: Red, Green, Purple

The three cards must display that feature as either all the same, or all different
